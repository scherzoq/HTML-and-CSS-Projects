Tennis Court Surfaces

GRASS

Grass is the surface that tennis was originally played on. Grass courts are the fastest courts in common use. Low bounces make for shorter points.

CLAY

Clay courts slow the ball down and produce a higher bounce. Since clay is the softest tennis surface, it is easier on the body than other surfaces.

HARDCOURT

Hard courts are faster than clay but slower than grass. They are the most common surface in profesional tennis.
